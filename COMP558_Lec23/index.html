How Do We Align Different Views?

Iterative Closest Points (ICP)

  • to register 2 sets of geometric enetities in 3D

Assumptions:

  • related by a rigid transformation (a rotation & translation)

Algorithm

  1. for each S1S_1S1​﻿, the closest point in Euclidean distance is chosen from S2S_2S2​﻿
    • if this distance is beyond a certain tolerance, that point in S1S_1S1​﻿ is ignored
  1. Then the avg of Euclidean distances between the matched points is 

1. compute correspondence
2. compute cross-convariance of matrice K 
		& mean values of each correpondence dataset (P,Q)

3. find rotation & translation -> update data
		[U, S, V] = svd(K)
				R = U*V*R_prev
				T = Qmean - R*Pmean
		-> find R and T
4. compute error


5. the process iterates till the average point-to-point error below a threshold.
