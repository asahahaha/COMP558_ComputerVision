3. Canny Edge Detection (1986)

STEP 1: compute gradient at EACH pixel

  • gradient of gaussian → take gaussian and then 1st derivative

  • mark a pixel as a candidate edge

    if gradient magnitude is greater than some threshold τhigh\tau_{high}τhigh​﻿

这样不太够

STEP 2: “non-maximum suppression”

  • compare gradient magnitude from STEP 1 to the neighbor who are nearest to the direction of that gradient!!!!
  • Eliminate edge pixels if magnitude of gradient is NOT a local maximum relative to neighbours

STEP 3: “hysteresis” thresholding

  • for each edge pixel survives STEP 2,
    • search neighbours in 2 opposite directions perpendicular to gradient
    • and find a chain of pixels whose gradient mag are all greater than threshold τlow\tau_{low}τlow​﻿
      • consider pixels in such chain to be edge pixels too

Additional notes on Canny:

👍

  1. should accurately estimate location of edges
