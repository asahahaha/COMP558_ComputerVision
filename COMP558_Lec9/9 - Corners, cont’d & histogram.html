Issues with Histograms

SOLUTION: Histogram Equalization

  • try to bit all intensities and stretch out the bins…
  • much smaller difference (smaller contrast) → stretch out intensities, when there is not enought light when picture was taken

SOLUTION: Histogram of Oriented Gradients (HoG)

  • normalized local histograms of image gradient orientations in a dense grid

HoG STEPS:

  1. divide image window into small blocks (cells)
  1. Within each cell compute 1D histogram using image gradients at each pixel

  • for invariance to illumination changes, shadowing….
    • normalize all cell to larger region (a block) by computing a measure of histogram energy
  • tile the detection window with denser overlapping grid of HoG descriptors
  • bigger window or smaller window? u decide

example of elfel tower, regardless of contrast and color

  • opposite to CNN
  • scale invariance
  • rotation invariance
  • translation invariance
  • illumination invariance
